# Project Brief: Beyond the Badge - Author & Consulting Platform

## 1. Project Overview
A high-fidelity digital platform for **Sereen Morgan-Murray**, a former FDA Investigator turned Global GxP Consultant. The site serves as both a book launch platform for her memoir/playbook, *Beyond the Badge*, and a professional portfolio for her consulting services, **SMM Arete GxP Consulting**.

## 2. Creative North Star: "The Investigative Editorial"
The visual identity moves away from generic author sites toward a "digital archive" or "case file" aesthetic. It evokes authority, precision, and deep industry knowledge through a sophisticated, high-contrast dark theme.

## 3. Brand Identity & Design System
*   **Brand Name:** Sereen Morgan-Murray / Beyond the Badge
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Primary:** Deep Hunter Green (#1B2B26) – Evokes stability and depth.
    *   **Typography/Text:** Antique White / Cream (#F5EEDA) – High legibility and classic feel.
    *   **Accent:** Burnished Gold (#C5A059) – Symbolizes authority and excellence.
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headlines:** Newsreader (Serif) – Editorial, authoritative, and timeless.
    *   **Body:** Manrope (Sans-serif) – Modern, clean, and professional.
*   **Visual Language:** Grid-based layouts, subtle textures (paper/grain), investigative iconography (dossier icons, badges), and high-contrast photography.

## 4. Site Architecture & Screens
1.  **Home Page:** Hero section "The Hook," "The Transition" problem/solution narrative, and the "Three Pillars of Regulatory Consulting."
2.  **Buy Book & Checkout:** Product landing page for *Beyond the Badge* featuring a secure, integrated order entry system for the hardcover editorial edition.
3.  **About the Author:** Detailed bio "The Investigative Mind," highlighting Sereen's FDA BIMO background and transition to advisory leadership.
4.  **Blog (Insights & Advisory):** A repository of professional dossiers and tactical articles (e.g., "The Auditor's Eye," "Decoding GxP").
5.  **Contact Us:** Secure communication portal for regulatory consultation and media inquiries.
6.  **Checkout (Final Authorization):** A dedicated, high-security transaction screen designed as an "Intelligence Manifest."

## 5. Key Functional Requirements
*   **E-commerce:** Secure order processing for physical books with shipping and tax calculations.
*   **Lead Generation:** Consulting inquiry form with organizational/affiliation tracking.
*   **Newsletter:** "The Investigative Circle" subscription for monthly regulatory insights.
*   **Content Management:** Categorized blog system for regulatory strategy, GxP compliance, and professional transitions.

## 6. Target Audience
*   FDA and government professionals transitioning to the private sector.
*   GxP and Quality leaders in the pharmaceutical and medical device industries.
*   Biotech startups seeking high-level regulatory strategy and inspection readiness.
